**Goal:** Prove that one real same-session sales path stays coherent from live coaching through final report/replay conclusions on the current learner/runtime/report/replay route family, without inventing a second authority or a frontend-only family map.
**Demo:** After this: After this: one real localhost sales session can be followed from live coaching on `/practice/{sessionId}` through `/practice/{sessionId}/report` and `/practice/{sessionId}/replay`, and the issue/goal family stays coherent on that same session.
